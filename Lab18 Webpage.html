ExperienceIT is a game-changing partnership between leading tech companies, workforce agencies and educational institutions - working together to teach, train and prepare the next generation of IT professionals in Detroit. Focus alignment between business, educational institutions and students; establishing Southeast Michigan as an internationally recognized and respected technology hub. Develop and execute continued educational training and practical experience for aspiring IT professionals; delivering alignment between the region’s information technology employers and curricula taught by participating academic institutions.
